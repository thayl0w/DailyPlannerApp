Help & User Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to Daily Planner! This guide will help you make the most of your planning experience.

The planner consists of three main areas:

  • Left Sidebar: Shows your current hour's notes and tasks
  • Calendar Grid: Click any day to plan your schedule
  • Monthly Summary: View statistics for the current month

Using the Calendar

Adding Notes

  1. Click on any day in the calendar
  2. Click the "üìù Notes" button
  3. Write your note in the text area
  4. Optionally add:
    • Color tags (Blue for School, Red for Urgent, Green for Personal)
    • Emoji icons
    • Checklist items
    • Image attachments
    • Reminder notifications
  5. Click "Save" to store your note

Creating Daily Plans

  1. Click on any day in the calendar
  2. Click the "üìÖ Plan" button (or just click the day)
  3. Choose your planning mode:
    • Plan: Fill hourly time slots from 12 AM to 11 PM
    • Notes: Write general daily notes
    • Tasks: Create a to-do list with checkboxes
  4. Mark tasks as urgent using the üö® button
  5. Click "Save" to store your plan

Current Hour Sidebar

The left sidebar automatically shows information for the current hour:

  • Current Hour: Displays the current time
  • Notes: Shows notes you've written for this hour
  • Tasks: Lists all incomplete tasks for today
  • Urgent Tasks: Highlights tasks marked as urgent in red

The sidebar updates automatically every hour and minute, so you always see what's relevant right now.

Features

Search Functionality

Use the search bar at the top to find notes and tasks across all months. Matching dates will be highlighted in yellow.

Dark Mode

Click the "üåô Dark Mode" button in the header to switch between light and dark themes. Your preference is saved automatically.

Themes

Choose from four themes:

  • Minimal: Clean, simple design
  • Neon: Vibrant gradient colors
  • Pastel: Soft, calming colors
  • Dark Glass: Modern glassmorphism effect

Timeline View

Click "üìÖ Timeline View" to see all your notes and plans in a chronological list, sorted by date.

Drag and Drop

Drag any calendar day to another day to move notes between dates.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Use color tags to categorize your notes (School, Urgent, Personal)
  • Mark important tasks as urgent so they appear in the red section
  • Plan your day hour by hour for better time management
  • Set reminders for important events or deadlines
  • Use the search function to quickly find past notes
  • Check the monthly summary to track your productivity

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Double-click a calendar day: Quickly open Notes modal
  • Single-click a calendar day: Open Daily Plan modal
  • Click Notes/Plan buttons: Open specific modal directly

Data Storage

All your notes, plans, and tasks are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your data stays private on your computer
  • No internet connection required after initial load
  • Data persists between browser sessions
  • Note: Clearing browser data will delete your notes

Troubleshooting

Notes not appearing?

Make sure you clicked "Save" after entering your note. Check the sidebar for current hour's content.

Sidebar not updating?

The sidebar updates automatically. If it seems stuck, refresh the page.

Can't find a note?

Use the search bar to search across all months. Check the Timeline View for a chronological list.

Reminders not working?

Make sure you've allowed browser notifications when prompted. Check your browser's notification settings.
